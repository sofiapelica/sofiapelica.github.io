Bio

Sofia Pelica studies thinking and feeling processes in social situations. She is doing research on adaptive memory and contamination for her Master’s thesis at Iscte – University Institute of Lisbon, where she gained a Bachelor's in Psychology. Her interests in cross-cultural research, psychological methods, and open science practices have led her to become a member of the Junior Researcher Programme. This hub for international collaborative research projects in psychology and policy is partnered with Columbia University and the University of Cambridge. The fact that she grew up in a cult is the basis for her motivation to share science and knowledge as her life mission.
