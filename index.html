Bio

Sofia Pelica studies thinking and feeling processes in social situations. She is doing research on adaptive memory and fear of contamination for her master's thesis at Iscte – University Institute of Lisbon, where she gained a bachelor's degree in Psychology. Her passion for psychological methods, open science practices, and cross-cultural research led her to become a member of the Junior Researcher Programme — a hub for international collaborative research projects in psychology and policy partnered with Columbia University and the University of Cambridge. Such interests stem from the vicissitudes of growing up in a cult, which is the basis for her motivation to share science and knowledge as her life mission.
